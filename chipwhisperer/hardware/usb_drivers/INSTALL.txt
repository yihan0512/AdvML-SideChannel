The attached USB drivers should work on any Windows system, both 32 and 64-bit. They are signed by NewAE Technology Inc.

To install them:
 1) Open your Device Manager.
 2) Find the device in the list, it should appear with a yellow exclamation mark under "Other devices".
 3) Double-click on the device.
 4) Hit "Update Driver"
 5) Select "Browse my computer for driver software".
 6) Point the wizard to this folder, ensure the "look in subfolders" option is selected.
